About

Joung Yunsu is a media artist based in Seoul, South Korea. Working at the intersection of digital technology and contemporary art, he creates immersive experiences through installation, performance, and audiovisual work.

His practice explores the poetic potential of data, light, and sound transforming digital processes into sensory, human experiences.
